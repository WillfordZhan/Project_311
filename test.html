Chris has been a leader in web design and development for the last two decades and now brings his experience to Faculty, a full-service digital agency.

He co-founded and hosted the beloved Brooklyn Beta for five years and has spoken at Webstock, OSCON, and South by Southwest. He’s also the author of two critically-acclaimed and frequently-cited books: Essential PHP Security and HTTP Developer’s Handbook.

He lives with his wife and three children in Boulder, Colorado. Ask him about soccer and backcountry adventures.
